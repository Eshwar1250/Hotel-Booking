Hotel Info

This is an ideally located hotel with unbeatable access to all major attractions in London and connections with all major cities in the UK, for those travelling on a budget but who wish to stay in the centre of London. A fast train connects Paddington Station (right opposite the hotel) directly with Heathrow Airport. Please note: breakfast is served in the hotel's restaurant next door. (0309/VM)

Location

The hotel is located right opposite Paddington Underground and Railway Station. This hotel enjoys a fantastic location with connections to all major tourist attractions that London offers.

Distance

  • 15 kms to the nearest airport (heathrow)
  • 1 minute walk to the nearest metro station (paddington)
  • Close to the station (paddington)
  • 1 minute walk to the nearest bus stop
  • 2 km to the nearest fair site (olympia or earls court)

Rooms

The rooms are small and are in fair condition. All the rooms at the hotel and the apartments at the annex building (sometimes hotel's guests are accommodated in the apartments) have been decorated in cream and maroon colours, dark wooden furniture and maroon carpets. They all have satellite TV, hair dryer, direct dial telephones, complimentary tea/coffee making facilities and wireless Internet access. The bathrooms are small and in fair condition. All bathrooms are equipped with baths and shower heads. Single rooms have walk-in showers only.

Restaurant

The restaurant offers breakfast, lunch, dinner, and drinks throughout the day. It's fully licensed, air conditioned and very modern.

Exterior

It's a traditional Victorian building with a white stucco facade and elaborate wrought iron balconies.

Lobby

The lobby and reception are very small but practical. A tiny business centre for one user only is located right at the entrance of the building, with limited sitting area.
